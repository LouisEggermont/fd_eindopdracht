## **AI Photobooth Flutter** | Smart App Development
### **2 MCT 2024**

<MdxBody>

Photobooth Flutter is a remake of our earlier <Link href="/projects/ai-photobooth">AI photobooth project</Link>, rebuilt as a standalone Flutter application. The app guides users through a complete kiosk-style flow: taking a picture, choosing a theme, filling in their details, waiting for AI-generated results, selecting their favourite images, and receiving them by email. My main focus was the frontend experience and the full user journey, while the app communicated with a backend API for image upload, prompt selection, status checking, result loading, and email sending. This remake allowed me to improve the original concept, create a more polished interface, and gain more experience with Flutter, animations, camera integration, and API-driven user flows.
